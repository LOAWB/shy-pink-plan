The size 8 edge.

Her size is actually a strategic advantage. Here is how to play it.

Conversion
W 8 = M 6.5

Jordan 1 women's-to-men's standard offset is 1.5 sizes. Shy Pink runs in men's sizing (M 4 to M 14 plus preschool), so M 6.5 is your target on every raffle and resale platform.

Retail Odds
Above average

M 6.5 sits below the most-contested band (M 8 to M 10). On hyped Travis releases, smaller sizes consistently fill at lower competition rates. Friendlier raffle math than the average entrant.

Resale Floor
$50-$100 less

Smaller sizes resell at a slight discount to hot-zone men's sizes because the buyer pool is smaller. Plan for $400 to $550 fill rather than the $500 to $700 men's-mid range.

Size strategy in one line

Enter every raffle at M 6.5. Bid resale at M 6.5. If a retailer offers women's sizing fields specifically (rare for this drop), use Women's 8.

If unsure between two sizes

Travis Scott AJ1 Lows generally fit true to size. If she is between W 7.5 and W 8, go W 8 (M 6.5). If between W 8 and W 8.5, also go W 8. The Low silhouette is forgiving and lacing tightens the midfoot.

Verify before bidding. Have her try on any Jordan 1 Low she or a friend owns. The 2026 Shy Pink uses the same last as past Travis AJ1 Lows. If a M 6.5 fits, you are locked. If not, adjust to M 7 (= W 8.5) or M 6 (= W 7.5).
